## Preliminary Groupings

### Group A: Deprecation clarity gap (pantry-review)
- Finding 1, Finding 2, Finding 3, Finding 7 — all stem from the same root cause: when `pantry-review` was deprecated and replaced by `fill-review-slots.sh`, the deprecation notice was added to the body of documents but not to headers or entry points (agent description, section title). Readers who land at those entry points do not immediately know the feature is obsolete.
- **Suggested combined fix**: Audit all entry points for `pantry-review` (agent description, section headers, table rows) and apply a consistent deprecation signal at the entry point level, not only in body text.

### Group B: Queen information diet violations (reviews.md placement)
- Finding 5 — standalone. The "After Consolidation Complete" section in reviews.md is structurally confusing because reviews.md is forbidden to the Queen, yet the section is nominally addressed to the Queen.

### Group C: Stale reference / typo

### Group D: Model assignment traceability
- Finding 6 — standalone. The "Set in big-head-skeleton.md" note for Nitpicker models is misleading about where the configuration actually lives.
